Meet Our Director

Manhar Prajapati

Director, Gateway Innotech Private Limited

Manhar Prajapati, M.Com, is an accomplished IT Professional with over a decade of experience since 2013. Under his visionary leadership, Gateway Innotech Private Limited continues the trusted legacy of Gateway Infosys, delivering enterprise-grade IT infrastructure, networking, security, and managed support solutions across India.

His expertise spans multiple technology domains including server infrastructure, network security, surveillance systems, and cloud integration. With a client-first approach and commitment to excellence, Manhar has built strong business relationships across multiple industries and states.

Our Journey

Company History

From Gateway Infosys to Gateway Innotech - A legacy of trust and innovation.

2013

The Beginning

Manhar Prajapati started his IT professional journey, laying the foundation for what would become a leading technology solutions provider in Gujarat.

2015

Gateway Infosys Established

Founded Gateway Infosys to provide computer sales, service, and basic networking solutions to local businesses in Ahmedabad.

2018

Expansion into Enterprise Solutions

Expanded services to include server installation, firewall security, CCTV surveillance, and enterprise networking infrastructure.

2020

Multi-Industry Presence

Started serving diverse industries including hotels, casinos, textiles, transport, and manufacturing sectors across Gujarat.

2023

Pan-India Operations

Extended services to 5+ states in India, becoming a recognized name in enterprise IT infrastructure and security solutions.

2026

Gateway Innotech Private Limited

Evolved from Gateway Infosys to Gateway Innotech Private Limited (CIN: U58201GJ2026PTC175714), marking a new era of corporate excellence and expanded capabilities.
