Our Story

Who We Are

Gateway Innotech Private Limited is a professionally managed IT infrastructure company headquartered in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. Incorporated under the Companies Act 2013 (CIN: U58201GJ2026PTC175714), we specialise in delivering end-to-end technology solutions for businesses of every size.

From supplying genuine hardware to designing, installing and maintaining complete IT ecosystems — servers, networks, security systems and cloud platforms — we are the single point of contact for all your technology needs.

Our team of certified engineers brings deep expertise across industries including hotels, casinos, textile mills, transport companies, manufacturing plants and corporate offices across Gujarat and beyond.

Our Journey

Growing With Our Clients

From a small IT service shop to a full-scale enterprise IT solutions provider — our journey has been defined by the trust our clients have placed in us.

2009

Founded in Ahmedabad

Started as a computer sales and repair service centre in Shahibaug, catering to local businesses and residents.

2012

Entered Networking & Servers

Expanded services to include structured cabling, server installation and networking for SME clients in Gujarat.

2016

Hotel & Hospitality Sector

Became a preferred IT partner for hotels in Ahmedabad. Deployed full IT infrastructure for 50+ properties.

2020

Cloud & Security Practice

Launched dedicated cloud migration and cybersecurity practice. Added Sophos and Cisco firewall solutions.

2024

Incorporated as Private Limited

Formally incorporated as Gateway Innotech Private Limited (CIN: U58201GJ2026PTC175714) to scale operations.

2025

500+ Clients Milestone

Crossed 500 active clients. Expanded team to 20+ engineers. Pan-Gujarat service coverage achieved.
